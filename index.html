Welcome to Our Institute


About Us

In an environment characterized by the generalization of training professionalizing to the whole of the Tunisian university, the Higher Institute of Technological Studies de Rades (ISET de Rades) is obliged to differentiate, diversify and enhance its training offer and able to consecrate the school as a flagship institution of education technological superior in Tunisia. The launch of this institute therefore comes at the right time to promote active, oriented and meaningful training to best meet the real needs of the environment industrial. It is a collegial reflection on the strategic orientations put forward in order to sustain the development of the school and to broaden its influence on its regional, national and even international environment.

Economics and Management

Presentation

The economics and management sciences department brings together all the training of the tertiary sector demanded by the market.

Specialties

  • Management
  • Accounting
  • Trade and distribution

Mechanical Engineering

Presentation

Mechanics translates into all the techniques for designing and manufacture everyday consumer items, machines, tools and other production systems.

Laboratories

  • Hydraulics and Pneumatics
  • Fluid mechanics
  • Laboratoire
  • NDT Non Destructive Testing and Quality Control
  • Computer Laboratory

Electrical Engineering

Presentation

The Electrical Engineering Department has trained senior technicians in all areas of systems industries that depend primarily on electrical energy for production and supply.

Specialties

  • Industrial mechanisms and media
  • Industrial Electricity Major
  • Maintenance of electrical systems
  • Industrial Security and Surveillance

Civil Engineering

Presentation

The Department of Civil Engineering started in September 1996. It brings together teachers and specialist equipment, fully available to students preparing for an L.A.GC (License Applied in Civil Engineering).

Horizons

  • Continuation of Studies
  • Business creation

IT technologies

Presentation

The Department of Information Technologies started in February 2003. It brings together teachers and professionals (temporary workers) available to students preparing for a D.E.S.T or an Applied License.

Opportunities

  • Application Developer
  • Web developer
  • Network and systems administrator
  • Operations IT
  • Operations Manager
